SECTION 1 — COMPANY OVERVIEW
================================================================================

About us
Riverside Homewares is an independent retail store based in Pune, Maharashtra. We specialize in kitchenware, table linens, bath textiles, lighting, and compact furniture for urban apartments. The business was founded in 2019 by siblings Meera and Arjun Kulkarni. Our registered trade name is Riverside Homewares; legal entity for invoices is Kulkarni Retail Pvt Ltd, GSTIN format as printed on each bill.

Mission and values
We aim to stock durable, well-designed goods at fair mid-market prices. We prioritize suppliers who can provide spare parts or clear warranty paths for electrical goods. We do not sell counterfeit branded goods. Our tagline is "Quality for everyday homes."

Store footprint
Primary showroom: ground floor, 42 MG Road, Camp, Pune 411001. There is no second branch. We do not operate franchise locations. All online orders ship from the Pune warehouse attached to the showroom.

SECTION 3 — SHIPPING AND FULFILLMENT
================================================================================

Geographic coverage
We ship only within the Republic of India. We do not export. We do not ship to PO boxes or army field post offices; a full street address with pin code is required.

Carriers
Standard orders use surface partners (names rotate by season; tracking link is in the confirmation email). Express metro service uses air-capable partners where available.

Timelines
Standard delivery: five to seven business days after payment clears in our bank or payment gateway. Express delivery (metros only): two to three business days after payment clears, subject to inventory confirmation. Business days exclude Sundays and public holidays at either origin (Pune) or destination hub.

Cut-off times
Orders paid before 2:00 PM IST on a business day typically enter the same-day packing queue. Orders after cut-off roll to the next business day.

Damaged in transit
Customer must report outer carton damage to the carrier when possible and email us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the box and item. We file claims with the carrier and either replace the item or refund once the claim is accepted.

SECTION 4 — RETURNS, REFUNDS, EXCHANGES
================================================================================

General return window
Unused items in original, unopened retail packaging may be returned within fourteen calendar days of the delivery timestamp shown on the carrier tracking. Refund goes to the original payment method only.

Opened or used goods
Opened or used items are not eligible for change-of-mind returns. Exception: manufacturing defect as defined below.

Defective merchandise
If an item is defective on arrival (e.g. lamp will not power on, pan coating visibly flaked before use), the customer must contact us within seven days of delivery with clear photos or a short video. We issue a prepaid return label for eligible pin codes, or arrange pickup in Pune city limits at no charge to the customer.

Exchanges
Size or color exchanges are treated as return plus new order if the original item is still in sellable condition within the fourteen-day window. If the replacement SKU costs more, the customer pays the difference; if less, we refund the difference after inspection.

Non-returnable categories
For hygiene reasons, opened bedding unless defective, opened personal care accessories bundled with textiles, and any item missing its serial sticker (where applicable) are final sale.

================================================================================
SECTION 5 — PAYMENTS AND INVOICING
================================================================================

Online payments
We accept UPI apps, major Indian debit and credit cards, and net banking through our payment gateway. We do not accept cash on delivery for website orders.

In-store payments
Showroom accepts cash, Indian cards, and UPI. We do not accept foreign currency.

GST and invoices
GST invoices are emailed as PDF after purchase. B2B customers needing GSTIN on the invoice must enter it at checkout before payment; retroactive invoice correction is allowed only within the same calendar month and requires finance approval.

================================================================================
SECTION 6 — WARRANTY AND SERVICE
================================================================================

Electrical goods
Desk lamps, rechargeable lights, and small appliances carry a twelve-month manufacturer warranty from the date of purchase on the invoice. Warranty is void if the serial label is removed, if unauthorized repair is attempted, or if water ingress is evident on non-waterproof models.

Kitchen metal and cookware
Knives, steel cookware, and non-electric gadgets have no express warranty beyond the fourteen-day return policy unless a manufacturing defect is documented within seven days of delivery.

Repairs
We do not operate an in-house repair shop. For warranty claims we coordinate with the brand service center or replace the unit per brand policy.

SECTION 12 — TROUBLESHOOTING (PRODUCT)
================================================================================

Desk lamp SKU RW-LAMP-DESK will not turn on
Check mains outlet with another device. Ensure adapter is fully seated. Try long-press on power touch panel for three seconds. If still dead within seven days of delivery, start defect workflow with photos.

Non-stick pan surface looks cloudy after first wash
Minor haze from hot water and oil can look like damage; if food still releases and there is no flaking, it is usually normal. If coating flakes, defect workflow.

USB port on lamp charges phone slowly
The USB-A port is auxiliary (typically 5W class); it is not a fast charger. Do not use it for laptops.

A: Check email for split-shipment notice. If no notice, email support with order ID within forty-eight hours.

Q: Wrong item shipped.

Q: Price dropped after I bought.
A: We do not retroactively match promotional prices unless the purchase was within twenty-four hours and the promotion was already public on site at purchase time — requires manager approval.
